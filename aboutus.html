Our Tools

Our flagship tool is an advanced Applicant Tracking System (ATS) that simplifies the recruitment process, enabling organizations to hire talented individuals whose skills and qualifications align perfectly with their requirements. With our ATS, businesses can efficiently manage job postings, track applications, and identify the best candidates for their teams.

In addition to our ATS, TAAEPA offers predictive analysis capabilities that help organizations anticipate employee turnover. By analyzing various factors and trends, our system can provide insights into which employees are at risk of leaving, allowing businesses to take proactive measures to retain them.

One of the key features of TAAEPA is our ability to provide complete details of each employee, including their personal information, job history, performance metrics, and more. This comprehensive view allows businesses to effectively manage their workforce and make informed decisions about resource allocation and talent development.

Furthermore, TAAEPA provides detailed analytics for each employee, enabling organizations to gain valuable insights into individual performance, engagement levels, and areas for improvement. This data-driven approach empowers businesses to optimize their HR strategies and maximize the potential of their workforce.
